This is a bingo card for The Binding of Isaac. When you get the 5 squares in any row, column, or diagonal, you win. You can do this across different runs.

When a square is the name of an item, it means you have to get that item, not just see it.

To "satisfy" means to use something until it gives you a reward and can never give rewards again. (usually when it explodes or disappears)

To "full clear" a floor means to clear all the rooms with no map icon and to kill the boss. You don't have to do challenge rooms or enter shops if you don't want to.

The "eyeball items" are ones that have a single eyeball prominently featured in the art for the icon before you pick them up.

"Orbitals" are the items that rotate very closely around you and that you can only have 3 of.
